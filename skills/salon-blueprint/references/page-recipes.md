# Salon page recipes

## Minimum fast-build site

- Home: outcome-led hero, key services, proof/gallery preview, why choose us, location/hours, closing action
- Services: categorized menu with confirmed prices/durations and booking actions
- Contact: phone, WhatsApp, map/directions, hours, inquiry form or verified booking link

## Full brochure site

Add About, Team, Gallery, FAQs, policies, and service-detail pages only when content or SEO strategy supports them.

## Conversion placement

Place the primary action in the header, hero, after service proof, and final CTA. On mobile, use a non-obstructive sticky action when appropriate. A booking action must lead to a real provider or working flow.

## Common salon variants

- Hair salon: cuts, colour, styling, treatments; transformation gallery
- Barbershop: cuts, fades, beard services; fast repeat booking
- Nail studio: manicure, pedicure, nail art, extensions; category gallery
- Spa: treatments, duration, preparation/aftercare, gift cards if verified
- Lash/brow: service suitability, maintenance intervals, policies
