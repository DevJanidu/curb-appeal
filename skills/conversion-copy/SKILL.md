# Conversion Copy

- Lead with the customer's desired outcome and local relevance, then explain the service.
- Use specific, natural language. Remove filler such as “welcome to,” “best-in-class,” and unverified superlatives.
- Keep one primary CTA label consistent; use a lower-friction secondary CTA.
- Make service sections answer: what it is, who it helps, what to expect, and the next step.
- Add useful objection-handling FAQs without inventing policies.
- Write unique page titles and meta descriptions based on verified business name, service, and location.
- Mark missing facts as `[Business detail needed: ...]`; never fabricate them.

## Hero formula

Eyebrow for category/location → outcome-led H1 → one-sentence differentiator → primary CTA → secondary CTA → concise trust cue.
